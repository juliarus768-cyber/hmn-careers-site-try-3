About Julia Cher

Founder-led agency model

Hire Me Now is led by Julia Cher and focused on career strategy and job search support for the Canadian labour market.

Our work goes beyond resume edits: we help clients clarify direction, sharpen positioning, and build a sustainable search strategy.

Supported by experienced counsellors

Julia works alongside multilingual career counsellors who provide practical support, accountability, and guidance through each stage of the process.

Language support includes Russian, Spanish, Hebrew, Cantonese, Bengali, Hindi, and Urdu.

Our approach

We combine strategic clarity with hands-on execution. Every client receives guidance tailored to their background, goals, and current challenges in the Canadian market.
